Participating Teams

32 nations competed for football's ultimate prize in Qatar. From defending champions France to debutants Qatar, each team brought their unique style and passionate supporters to the tournament.

Qatar

Group A

Host nation making their World Cup debut. Despite elimination in the group stage, Qatar impressed with their organization and passion.

Star Player: Almoez Ali

Ecuador

Group A

La Tricolor reached the Round of 16 with disciplined performances and strong team spirit under Gustavo Alfaro.

Star Player: Enner Valencia

Senegal

Group A

The African champions progressed to the knockout stages, showing resilience despite missing injured star Sadio Mané.

Star Player: Kalidou Koulibaly

Netherlands

Group A

The Dutch topped their group and reached the quarter-finals, playing attractive football under Louis van Gaal.

Star Player: Virgil van Dijk

England

Group B

The Three Lions reached the quarter-finals with impressive attacking displays, led by Harry Kane's clinical finishing.

Star Player: Harry Kane

Iran

Group B

Team Melli showed determination and skill, creating several memorable moments including a famous victory over Wales.

Star Player: Mehdi Taremi

USA

Group B

The young American squad impressed by reaching the Round of 16, showing the bright future of US soccer.

Star Player: Christian Pulisic

Wales

Group B

The Dragons returned to the World Cup after 64 years, with Gareth Bale leading an emotional campaign.

Star Player: Gareth Bale

Argentina

Group C - WORLD CHAMPIONS

Led by Lionel Messi, Argentina overcame early setbacks to claim their third World Cup title in dramatic fashion.
